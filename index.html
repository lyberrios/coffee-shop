Learn more about us

Since 1939, our café has been a meeting point for lovers of good coffee and good conversation. With decades of tradition, we pride ourselves on offering a unique experience, where every detail is designed to make you feel at home. Over the years, we've perfected the art of preparing different types of coffee: from the most intense espresso to smooth and aromatic combinations that invite you to relax. But our flavor doesn't end there. We also offer a special selection of filled baguettes, made with fresh and flavorful ingredients that perfectly complement any moment of the day. Here, every cup tells a story, and every visit is a new opportunity to share it with you.
